@mig encouraged me to hop on the 'what I've been listening to' train, so here are the five albums I've been bumping to kick off the year. I'm gonna use my stats.fm chart to count this, as opposed to simply picking and choosing, so I'm prepared to be outed


Saoirse Dream 

this was my other favourite album of last year

Underscores - Wallsocket

man, I slept on this album for way too long. I've had underscores circling around my playlists for a while, but late last year I finally decided to listen to Wallsocket in full. this is pretty much the peak of anything hyperpop and it's not even close. taking from all across the genre spectrum and blending it into an expertly crafted concept album that somehow has me relating to small town American dystopia in ways I didn't expect.

Ninajirachi 

This was easily one of my favourite albums of last year, if not the absolute top of the list. It's dance music engineered in a lab to appeal to me specifically, as someone who spent more time in counter strike lobbies listening to monstercat than listening to dance music in its natural habitat, and making ass beats on a cracked copy of FL studio late into the night. An absolute tour-de-force of a record, with effectively a seamless first handful of songs that have kept me coming back over and over.
